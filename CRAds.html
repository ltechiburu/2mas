Consumer Research Ads

A collection of media created to recruit consumers for Starbucks consumer research studies. These were posted on various social media platforms, flyers around the city, and public transit stations.


Using this content, I was able to leverage a limited budget to launch recruitment campaigns that saw an increase in our consumer database of over 220%, to 5500 panelists.
